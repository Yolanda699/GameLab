Introduction

I am Yolanda, an undergraduate student in Communications and New Media at the National University of Singapore. My academic journey is shaped by a deep interest in game studies and game development, especially in understanding how interactive media can influence the way people think, feel, and connect with the world around them.

I believe that games hold powerful potential as a creative medium. They offer unique ways to tell stories, spark meaningful conversations, and encourage players to reflect on social issues. With this in mind, I hope to create experiences that weave cultural heritage into thoughtful and engaging gameplay.

My work is driven by curiosity and a desire to explore how digital media can preserve traditions, share diverse perspectives, and inspire communities. I am continually learning, experimenting, and collaborating to understand how design, technology, and storytelling can come together to create impact.
